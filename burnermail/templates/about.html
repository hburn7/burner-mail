Take Control of Your Privacy

Using a burner email address for websites is a great step to reduce cross-site tracking.

Why Use Burner E-Mails?

  • Privacy
  • Anonymity
  • Spam
  • Security

Privacy

No personally identifying information is linked to anyone who uses a burner E-Mail address. Take control of your digital identity.

Anonymity

Burner E-Mails allow anonymous sending and receiving of E-Mails.

Spam

Using one burner E-Mail per site account allows for manual force opt-outs of all E-Mail communications. Simply destroy the burner E-Mail to cease all communication.

Security

Our servers do not store content forwarded to your primary E-Mail address. We will never sell your data.
